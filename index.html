Doctrine

Compressed spine

We give yesterday a legible form.

Clarity without haste. Print first. Durable by default.

Negative doctrine

  • We do not publish prediction as authority.
  • We do not optimize for outrage, dunking, or belonging.
  • We do not compete on speed or hot takes.
  • We do not require belief—only attention.
  • We do not sacrifice clarity to be “timely.”

How we write

  • Concrete nouns
  • Verbs of use, not vision
  • Conditional phrasing (“holds if,” “fails when”)
  • Sources and verification paths
  • Templates and repeatable formats
